Ishan Uday scholarship for north eastern region(NER)

 Documents required:-  
1.Adhaar Card
2.Income certificate
3.Domicile Certificate
4.verified Mark sheet


 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Candidate should be a resident of Assam,Arunachal pradesh,manipur,Meghalaya,Mizoram,Nagaland and Tripura
•Annual income less than 4,50,000
•Transgenders can also apply for this scheme
•The candidates from open universities can not apply to this scheme
•The candidates belonging to management quota are not eligible to this scheme


 Steps:- 
1.Go to Official portal of website
2.Click on either New registration or Renewal application as per need
3.Complete the undertaking process by clicking on boxes
4.Noe fill the proper and correct details to generate student application ID
5.Put up the ID and enter the OTP in the corner you will find the application form in the left corner
6.Carefully Fill all the details in the form
7.Upload all the documents
8.Click on final submit button
