Ishan Uday Scholarship for North Eastern Region (NER)

Documents Required:

1. Aadhaar Card
2. Income Certificate
3. Domicile Certificate
4. Verified Mark Sheet

Eligibility Criteria:

• Candidate should be a resident of Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura.
• Annual income should be less than INR 4,50,000
• Transgenders can also apply for this scheme.
• Candidates from open universities cannot apply.
• Candidates belonging to the management quota are not eligible for this scheme.

Steps:

1. Go to the official portal of the website.
2. Click on either New Registration or Renewal application as per need.
3. Complete the undertaking process by clicking the appropriate boxes.
4. Fill in the correct details to generate the student application ID.
5. Enter the application ID and OTP to access the application form.
6. Carefully fill in all the details in the form.
7. Upload all required documents.
8. Click on the final submit button.
