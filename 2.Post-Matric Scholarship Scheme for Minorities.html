Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Minorities

 Doucuments required:-  
1.Domicile certificate
2.Self-declaration of minority community certificate by the student.
3.Self-attested copy of previous academic mark sheet.
4.Aadhar Card
6.Income certificate
7.Proof of bank account either of the student or joint account with mother/father.

 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Applicants must belong to a minority community as notified by the Government of India
(include Muslims, Sikhs, Christians, Buddhists, Zoroastrians (Parsis) and Jains).
•The candidates must be studying in class 11 or above.
•The students studying in classes 11, 12, graduate, postgraduate, technical or vocational courses, M.Phil or PhD are eligible to apply for this scholarship.
•Students should have scored a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent grades in the previous final examination.
•The annual family income should be less than INR 2 lakh from all the sources.


 Steps:-  
1.The applicant first needs to visit the official website of the NSP.
2.One then needs to register as a new user by providing all the required details.
3.Provide domicile details, name, date of birth, mobile number, gender, email ID and select the scholarship to apply.
4.Completing this step will generate an OTP. Use this OTP to verify the mobile number.
5.Use the OTP received on the registered mobile to log in on NSP and fill out the form.
6.This will generate an application ID and password that can be used for future references.
7.However, one must note that no changes in the application are possible after the submission of the application.
