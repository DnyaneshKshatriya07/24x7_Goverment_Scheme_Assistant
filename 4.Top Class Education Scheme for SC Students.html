Top Class Education Scheme for SC Students

 Doucuments Required:-  
1.Caste Certificate
2.Income Certificate
3.Bank account details of the institution and the student.
4.Proof of admission
5.Scorecard of entrance exam qualified(if applicable)
6.Fee structure of the program.

 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Family income from all the resources should be less than INR 8.00 lakh.
•This scheme is not applicable for more than 2 siblings in a family.
•Student must have studied their class X from any of the central/state/municipal government or government aided schools.
•30% of slots allotted to the institution shall be reserved for eligible SC Girl students.
•Having secured admission in a full-time course in any of the notified institutions according to general selection criteria.


 Steps:- 
1.Click on the 'Apply Now' button below.
2.Read the guidelines carefully, select the undertaking, and 'Continue'.
3.Select the state of domicile, scholarship category, scheme type (scholarship scheme), gender, and write the applicant's name, date of birth, mobile number, and email ID.
4.Provide the bank details (Bank name, IFSC Code, Account Number)
5.Select Aadhaar or Bank Account Number as identification detail and click on the 'Register' button.
6.The mobile number will be verified and an OTP will be generated.
7.Now, log in using OTP and fill in the required details in the form and submit.
