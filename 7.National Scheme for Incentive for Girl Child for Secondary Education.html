National Scheme for Incentive for Girl Child for Secondary Education

 Need Of Doucuments:- 


 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•All SC/ST girls who pass class VIII
•Girls, who pass class VIII examination from Kastrurba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (irrespective of whether they belong to Scheduled Castes or Tribes) Girls age should be below 16 years .
•Girls who are Married are ineligible.
•Girls studying in private un-aided schools and enrolled in schools run by Central Government are excluded.
•Student should be enrolled for class IX in State/UT Government, Government-aided or local body schools.


 Steps:- 
1.The application performa is available to District Education Officers or Principals of the schools in hard copy. It can also be downloaded from this link by the District Education Officer. It can be filled up by the student or school in excel sheet. The information of all the students of a school will be compiled in Excel sheet and verified by the Principal of the school, which will then be sent to District Education Officers either through email or on CD and hard copy.
2.The District Education Officer (DEO) will log in with user ID and Password generated by the State Nodal Officer and sent to a registered mobile number of DEO.
3.The mobile number can be given either of the student or parent/guardian/neighbor/friend etc.
4.The use of Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for data submission will be done by the State Nodal Officer only while submitting the data from State to this Department on NSP.
