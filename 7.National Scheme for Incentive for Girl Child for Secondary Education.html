National Scheme for Incentive for Girl Child for Secondary Education

Need of Documents:

Currently, no specific document requirements have been mentioned for this scheme.

Eligibility Criteria:

• All SC/ST girls who pass Class VIII.
• Girls who pass Class VIII from Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (irrespective of caste/tribe).
• Girls should be below 16 years of age.
• Married girls are ineligible.
• Girls studying in private unaided schools and those enrolled in Central Government schools are excluded.
• Students must be enrolled in Class IX in State/UT Government, Government-aided, or local body schools.

Steps:

1. The application proforma is available to District Education Officers or Principals in hard copy or can be downloaded by the District Education Officer.
2. The proforma can be filled by the student or school in an Excel sheet. The school's information must be verified by the Principal and sent to District Education Officers via email, CD, or hard copy.
3. The District Education Officer (DEO) logs in using a user ID and password provided by the State Nodal Officer.
4. The mobile number can belong to the student, parent, guardian, neighbor, or friend.
5. The State Nodal Officer uses a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for submitting data from the state to the department on NSP.
