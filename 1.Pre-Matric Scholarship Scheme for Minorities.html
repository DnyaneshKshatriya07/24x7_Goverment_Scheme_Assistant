Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Minorities

 Doucuments required :- 
1.Minority community certificate
2.Income certificate
3.Bank account details
4.Mark sheet of the previous exam
5.Aadhar card


 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Applicants must belong to a minority community as notified by the Government of India
(include Muslims, Sikhs, Christians, Buddhists, Zoroastrians (Parsis) and Jains).
•The candidates must be studying in class 1 to 10 in a government or private school.
•Students should have scored a minimum of 50% marks in the previous final examination.
•The annual family income should not exceed INR 1 lakh from all the sources.


 Steps:- 
1.Visit the official website of the NSP.
2.One then needs to register as a new user by providing all the required details.
3.Provide domicile details, name, date of birth, mobile number, gender, email ID and select the scholarship to apply.
4.Completing this step will generate an OTP that will be used to verify the mobile number.
5.Use the OTP received on the registered mobile to log in on NSP and fill the form.
6.This will generate an application ID and password that can be used for future references.
7.However, it must be noted that no changes can be made once the application has been submitted.
