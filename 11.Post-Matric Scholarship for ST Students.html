Post-Matric Scholarship for ST Students

 Doucuments Required:- 
1.A passport-size photograph
2.Scheduled Tribe certificate
3.Income certificate
4.A receipt in acknowledgement of the scholarship in the previous year

 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Family income should not exceed INR 2,50,000 lakh.
•All children of the same parents/guardian are eligible.
•ST candidates should have passed the class X examination/higher examination /higher secondary education from a recognized university or board of secondary education.
•Students studying through correspondence courses are eligible.
•Should not be enrolled in any other scholarship.

 Steps:-  
1.Click on the 'Apply Now' link below.
2.Navigate to 'Applicant Corner'and click on 'New Registration'.
3.Now click on 'Click here for other Scholarship Schemes hosted on NSP for AY 2022-23'.
4.Read the guidelines carefully, select the undertaking, and 'Continue'.
5.Select the state of domicile, scholarship category (Pre Matric), scheme type (scholarship scheme), gender, and write the applicant's name, date of birth, mobile number, and email ID.
6.Provide the bank details (Bank name, IFSC Code, Account Number)
7.Select Aadhaar or Bank Account Number as identification detail and click on the 'Register' button.
8.he mobile number will be verified and an OTP will be generated.
9.Now, log in using OTP and fill out the application form.
10.After successful submission of the application form, an application ID and password will be generated which can be used for future references.

Note:- All the applicants are advised to fill the form correctly, no changes can be made once submitted.
