Education of ST Students

Documents Required:

• Domicile Certificate
• Student Photograph
• Income Certificate issued by the competent authority
• Caste Certificate issued by the competent authority
• Academic Marksheet (attested by the institution)
• Proof of admission to the course
• Bank account details (Account number, IFSC code, and Bank name)

Steps to Apply:

1. Visit the official scholarship portal.
2. Navigate to the 'Applicant Corner' and click on 'New Registration'.
3. Carefully read the guidelines and accept the undertaking.
4. Fill in the required details like name, date of birth, contact information, and bank details.
5. Upload the necessary documents in the specified format.
6. Submit the application form.
7. Save the application ID and password for future reference.

Note: Ensure all information is accurate, as no changes can be made after submission.
