Maulana Azad National Scholarship for Girls

 Documents required:- 
1.Category Certificate.
2.Income certificate
3.Bank account details
4.Mark sheet of the previous exam
5.Recently clicked passport size photograph
6.Physically or disable certificate.
7.Aadhaar Card if not Adhaar then you can attach a birth certificate, BPL Card, or ration card


 Eligibility Criteria:- 
1.Only girl students belonging to six notified minority communities i.e Muslims, Christians, Buddhists, Janis, Parsis.
2.Minority girl students studying in class 9th to 12th and must have secured a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent in the previous class.
3.Annual Family income should be not more than 2 lakh PA.
4.Income certificate to be issued by competent authority declared by state govt/ UTs/ Administration. Income certificate in English/ Hindi/ Regional language.
5.Students can only submit an online application. If submitting more than one, it will be considered a duplicate and the application will be canceled.
6.Scholarship Amount will be credited directly to the beneficiary account.
7.The scholarship will be not be given to more than two students from the same family.
8.There is no registration fee for applying online form.


 Steps:-  
1. Applicants applying for the first time have to register themselves on the MAEF Scholarship portal by clicking “Student Registration Form” 2. While filling the registration form, the candidates are every mandatory detail.
3. Then submit the registration form.
4. After submitting the form, students are given a link to download the school verification form that needs to be checked by the school principal and uploaded while completing the scholarship.
5. Applicants needed to log in with their registration details.
6. Fill in the application details and upload the documents and submit the application
