Maulana Azad National Scholarship for Girls

Documents Required:

1. Category Certificate
2. Income Certificate
3. Bank Account Details
4. Mark Sheet of the Previous Exam
5. Recently Clicked Passport Size Photograph
6. Physically Disabled Certificate
7. Aadhaar Card (or Birth Certificate, BPL Card, or Ration Card)

Eligibility Criteria:

1. Only girl students belonging to six notified minority communities (Muslims, Christians, Buddhists, Jains, Parsis).
2. Minority girl students studying in class 9th to 12th and must have secured a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent in the previous class.
3. Annual family income should not exceed INR 2 lakh per annum.
4. Income certificate to be issued by competent authority declared by state govt/UTs/Administration.
5. Students can only submit an online application. If submitting more than one, it will be considered a duplicate, and the application will be canceled.
6. Scholarship Amount will be credited directly to the beneficiary's account.
7. The scholarship will not be given to more than two students from the same family.
8. There is no registration fee for applying online.

Steps:

1. Applicants applying for the first time have to register themselves on the MAEF Scholarship portal by clicking “Student Registration Form”.
2. While filling the registration form, candidates must complete every mandatory detail.
3. Then submit the registration form.
4. After submitting the form, students will receive a link to download the school verification form, which needs to be signed by the school principal and uploaded while completing the scholarship.
5. Applicants need to log in with their registration details.
6. Fill in the application details, upload the required documents, and submit the application.
