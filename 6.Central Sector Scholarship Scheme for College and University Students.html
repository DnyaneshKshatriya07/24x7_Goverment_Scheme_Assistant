Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for College and University Students

Documents Required:

1. Bank Passbook of the applicant
2. Income Certificate
3. Aadhaar Card (alternatives include Aadhaar enrollment ID slip, driving license, PAN, Passport)
4. Mark sheet of Class 12
5. Disability Certificate (if applicable)

Eligibility Criteria:

• Students must have scored more than 80% in Class 12 from recognized boards (SEB/ICSE/CBSE).
• Annual family income must be less than INR 8,00,000.
• Applicant must not be enrolled in any other scholarship scheme.
• The college/university must be recognized by AICTE, UGC, MCI, or DCI.
• Reserved categories receive the following reservations: SC - 15%, ST - 7.5%, OBC - 27%, PWD - 5%.

Steps:

1. Go to the official portal of the website.
2. Click on either "New Registration" or "Renewal Application" as needed.
3. Complete the undertaking process by selecting the relevant checkboxes.
4. Fill in the proper and correct details to generate the student application ID.
5. Enter the ID and OTP to access the application form.
6. Carefully fill in all the details in the form.
7. Upload all the required documents.
8. Click on the "Final Submit" button.
