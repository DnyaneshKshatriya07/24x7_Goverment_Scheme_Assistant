Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students

 Doucuments Required:- 
1.Caste certificate
2.Income certificate
3.Caste validity certificate
4.HSC or SSC marksheet or last examination mark sheet
5.Gap certificate (Mandatory in case of a gap)
6.Father/Guardians death certificate (if applicable)
7.Ration card
8.Leaving certificate
9.Declaration certificate of parents


 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Should be a resident of India.
•Should belong to the OBC community.
•Have an annual family income of less than or equal to INR 1 lakh.
•Be pursuing studies at post-matric level (Class 11 to PhD level).
•Have taken admission through CAP round (if applied for a professional course).
•Have a minimum of 75% attendance for current academic year.


 Steps:-  
1.Click on the 'Apply Now' button below.
2.Click on 'New Applicant Registration'.
3.Fill in all required details, verify the email and phone number.
4.Complete the Aadhaar authentication process either through OTP or biometrics.
5.The candidates will receive an OTP on their registered mobile number if they opt for OTP authentication.
6.Complete the registration process by filling in all the required details.
7.Log in using the username and password.
8.Complete the scholarship application, upload the necessary documents and submit.
