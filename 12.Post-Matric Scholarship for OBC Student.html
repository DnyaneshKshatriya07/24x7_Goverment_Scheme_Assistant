Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students

Documents Required:

1. Caste certificate
2. Income certificate
3. Caste validity certificate
4. HSC or SSC marksheet or last examination mark sheet
5. Gap certificate (Mandatory in case of a gap)
6. Father/Guardian's death certificate (if applicable)
7. Ration card
8. Leaving certificate
9. Declaration certificate from parents

Eligibility Criteria:

• Should be a resident of India.
• Should belong to the OBC community.
• Have an annual family income of less than or equal to INR 1 lakh.
• Be pursuing studies at post-matric level (Class 11 to PhD level).
• Have taken admission through CAP round (if applied for a professional course).
• Have a minimum of 75% attendance for the current academic year.

Steps:

1. Click on the 'Apply Now' button below.
2. Click on 'New Applicant Registration'.
3. Fill in all required details, verify email and phone number.
4. Complete Aadhaar authentication via OTP or biometrics.
5. The candidates will receive an OTP on their registered mobile number if OTP authentication is chosen.
6. Complete registration by filling in the required details.
7. Log in using the username and password.
8. Complete the scholarship application, upload documents, and submit.
