Merit-cum-Means Scholarship for Professional and Technical Courses CS

Documents Required:

1. Candidate's photograph
2. Domicile Certificate
3. Self Declaration of minority community certificate by the student
4. Self-attested copy of previous academic mark-sheet
5. Fee receipt of current course year
6. Scanned copy of Aadhaar Enrollment/Aadhaar Card
7. Income certificate issued by designated state/UT authority
8. Proof of bank account of the student or joint account with mother/father

Eligibility Criteria:

• Should belong to the minority communities (Muslims, Sikhs, Christians, Buddhists, Jain, and Parsis/Zoroastrians).
• Have an annual family income of less than INR 2,50,000 lakh.
• Should be pursuing a technical or professional course at the undergraduate or postgraduate level from a recognized institution.
• Should have secured at least 50% marks or equivalent grade in the previous final examination.

Steps:

1. Click on the 'Apply Now' button.
2. Click on 'New Registration'.
3. Now click on 'Click here for other Scholarship Schemes hosted on NSP for AY 2022-23'.
4. Read the guidelines carefully, select the undertaking, and 'Continue'.
5. Select the state and fill up all the details carefully and properly, also upload all the documents.
6. Provide all the bank details.
7. Select the identification details (Aadhaar or bank account number).
8. Verify mobile number to generate OTP.
9. Fill the OTP and complete the application form as shown in the video link below.
10. The application ID generated will be used for future reference.
