National Means Cum Merit Scholarship Scheme

Documents Required:

No documents are needed. However, you must have secured at least 55% marks or equivalent grades in the Class 8 examination. Additionally, you need at least 55% marks or equivalent grade in the Class 7 examination to appear for the selection test for the scholarship.

Eligibility Criteria:

• Family income from all resources should be less than INR 3,50,000 lakh.
• Must have a minimum of 55% marks or equivalent grade in Class VII examination.
• Student should be regular in Government, Government-aided, or any local school.
• NVS and KVS students are not eligible for this scheme.

Steps:

1. Click on the 'Apply Now' link below.
2. Navigate to 'Applicant Corner' and click on 'New Registration'.
3. Click on 'Click here for other Scholarship Schemes hosted on NSP for AY 2022-23'.
4. Read the guidelines carefully, select the undertaking, and 'Continue'.
5. Select the state of domicile, scholarship category (Pre Matric), scheme type (scholarship scheme), gender, and provide the applicant's details.
6. Provide bank details (Bank name, IFSC Code, Account Number).
7. Select Aadhaar or Bank Account Number as identification detail and click on the 'Register' button.
8. Verify the mobile number through OTP.
9. Log in using OTP and complete the application form.
10. After submission, note the application ID and password for future reference.

Note: All applicants are advised to fill the form carefully as no changes can be made once submitted.
