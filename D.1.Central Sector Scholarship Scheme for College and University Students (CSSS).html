Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for College and University students

 Documents required:- 
1.Bank Passbook of applicant
2.Income certificate
3.Adhaar card(Alternatives can be Adhar enrolment ID slip,driving license,PAN,Passport)
4.Mark sheet of class 12
5.Disability Certificate (if applicable)


 Eligibility Criteria:- 
•Students must have more than 80% in class 12th of concerned boards (SEB/ICSE/CBSE)
•Annual income less than 8,00,000
•Applicant must not be enrolled in any other scholarship Scheme
•The college/University must be recognized by AICTE,UGC,MCI,DCI
•The candidates belonging to reserved categories of SC, ST, OBC and PWD will get a reservation of 15%, 7.5%, 27% and 5%, respectively


 Steps:-  
1.Go to Official portal of website
2.Click on either New registration or Renewal application as per need
3.Complete the undertaking process by clicking on boxes
4.Noe fill the proper and correct details to generate student application ID
5.Put up the ID and enter the OTP in the corner you will find the application form in the left corner
6.Carefully Fill all the details in the form
7.Upload all the documents
8.Click on final submit button
