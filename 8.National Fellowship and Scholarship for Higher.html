National Fellowship for Higher Education of ST Students

Documents Required for Scholarship:

• Domicile certificate
• Student photograph
• Institute verification form/Bonafide certificate
• Income certificate issued by the competent authority
• Caste certificate issued by the competent authority
• Certificate of previous academic marksheet (attested by the Institute)
• Fee receipt of the current course year
• Proof of bank account in the name of the student

Eligibility Criteria:

• Students should have passed the postgraduate examination.
• Must be admitted or registered for a regular full-time M.Phil/Ph.D. degree program in institutions recognized by UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act/ICAR.
• Must fulfill the prescribed UGC/ICAR norms.

Steps:

1. Click on the 'Apply Now' link below.
2. Navigate to 'Applicant Corner' and click on 'New Registration'.
3. Click on 'Click here for other Scholarship Schemes hosted on NSP for AY 2022-23'.
4. Read the guidelines carefully, select the undertaking, and 'Continue'.
5. Select the state of domicile, scholarship category, scheme type, gender, and provide the applicant's name, date of birth, mobile number, and email ID.
6. Enter bank details (Bank name, IFSC Code, Account Number).
7. Select Aadhaar or Bank Account Number as the identification detail and click on 'Register'.
8. Verify the mobile number and complete OTP authentication.
9. Log in using OTP and fill out the application form.
10. Submit the application form successfully to generate an application ID and password for future references.

Note: All applicants must fill out the form correctly. No changes can be made once submitted.
